The Habit Burger Story – How It All Began

The Habit Burger Grill started its journey in 1969 in Santa Barbara, California. What began as a small, family-owned burger shop quickly gained popularity due to its unique open-flame grilling method and commitment to freshness. Over the years, Habit Burger has grown across the United States while maintaining the same dedication to high-quality ingredients and flavourful burgers. One of the most iconic creations is the Santa Barbara Char, made with fresh avocado, sourdough bread, and a perfectly flame-grilled patty. This burger represents the brand’s heritage and dedication to delicious, made-to-order meals. Today, Habit Burger is beloved for its consistent quality, variety of menu items, and welcoming atmosphere for families and burger lovers alike.
